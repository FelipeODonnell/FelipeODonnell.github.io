Privacy Policy

Updated 2024

Welcome to Apex Match AI

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients and their data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information related to our services tailored for real estate agents, including data about properties and clients, and the use of AI to enhance sales and workflow automation.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and any other information real estate agents choose to provide about themselves or their clients.
  • Information related to properties including location, pricing, and owner details, as provided by our clients.

b. Usage and Device Information

  • Information about how you access and use our services, including through our website and applications.
  • Device information, such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.

c. AI Data Processing

Data inputted into our AI systems for analysis, including sales data, client interactions, and property details to automate workflow and boost sales.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services, including AI analysis to offer insights and suggestions.
  • Communicate with you about your account, system updates, and service offerings.
  • Enhance the security and safety of our services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share information:

  • With service providers who support our business operations, such as cloud hosting and data analysis, under confidentiality agreements.
  • When legally required or to protect our rights and safety, and that of our users and third parties.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

4. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Withdraw consent for processing your data, where applicable.

6. International Transfers

We may transfer, process, and store your information in countries other than your own, which may have different data protection laws. By using our service, you consent to such transfers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at team@apexmatch.ai.
